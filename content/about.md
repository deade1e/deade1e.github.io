# Ciao!

I am Francesco Pompò, an independent IT Security Researcher and Developer.

I enjoy breaking stuff and seeing how it's made. I also like to produce
documentation about it.

Sometimes I break things that nobody cares about, and that sounds as funny as
it is.

I like to learn about stuff related to my field of interest, I can't help but
keep assimilating information about any matter that concerns IT security.
This, for example, made me rewrite the client portion of
[Tor](https://github.com/deade1e/lor) for a PoC and a [network scanner](https://github.com/deade1e/radar).

My knowledge makes me a good resource for my peers, as oftentimes I've already
faced a problem another person is working on.

Within the business context I often find myself spending more
time speaking with people than actually staying at the computer.

It's easy to tell if I am up to something. If my life is messy, then the
response is **yes**.
